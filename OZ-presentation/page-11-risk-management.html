10.1 Risk Management Framework

Risk Overview

OZ investments present unique risk profiles combining traditional investment risks with regulatory and tax-specific considerations.

Regulatory Risks

Compliance Failure: Loss of QOF status.

Mitigation: Professional monitoring.

Market Risks

Economic Cycles: Performance volatility.

Mitigation: Diversification strategies.

Liquidity Management

The 10-year holding period creates liquidity constraints requiring careful portfolio construction and capital planning.
