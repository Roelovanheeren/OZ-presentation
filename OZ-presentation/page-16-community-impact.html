13.2 Impact Measurement

Community Development

OZ investments generate measurable community benefits through job creation, infrastructure development, and economic revitalization.

Key Metrics

• Job creation and retention
• Capital deployment amounts
• Community infrastructure improvements

ESG Integration

Environmental, social, and governance factors increasingly influence OZ investment decisions and reporting standards.

Stakeholder Communication

Regular communication with community stakeholders, government officials, and program participants enhances project success and regulatory relationships.
