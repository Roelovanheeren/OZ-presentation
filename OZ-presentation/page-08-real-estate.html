7.1 Real Estate Investment Overview

Market Dominance Rationale

Real estate represents approximately 90% of OZ investments due to regulatory clarity, established operational frameworks, and favorable compliance characteristics.

Multifamily Residential

Multifamily development and repositioning dominate OZ real estate strategies, particularly in high-growth metropolitan areas with strong demographic tailwinds.

Preferred Property Types

• Build-to-Rent Single-Family
• Multifamily Development
• Mixed-Use Projects
• Industrial/Logistics

Development Strategy Advantages

Ground-up construction provides maximum regulatory flexibility as original use property, eliminating substantial improvement requirements and providing clearest path to compliance.
