7.2 Strategy Implementation

Geographic Focus

Successful real estate strategies concentrate on select high-growth markets with strong economic fundamentals and population growth.

Selection Criteria

• Population growth trends
• Job creation and economic diversity
• Infrastructure development
• Regulatory environment

Development Execution

Ground-up construction projects offer highest value creation through entitlement, construction, and lease-up phases while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Exit Strategy Planning

Multiple exit pathways including institutional sales, refinancing, or long-term hold strategies optimize the 10-year benefit period.
