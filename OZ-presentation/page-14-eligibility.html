12.2 Eligibility Requirements

Capital Gains Requirement

Investors must have realized capital gains from the sale of capital assets to a third party to be eligible for OZ tax benefits.

Eligible Gain Sources

• Stock sales
• Real estate sales
• Business sales
• Partnership interests

Investment Timeline

Capital gains must be invested in a QOF within 180 days of the gain recognition date to qualify for tax benefits.

Mixed Funds

Investors may combine eligible capital gains with other funds, but only the capital gains portion receives tax benefits.
