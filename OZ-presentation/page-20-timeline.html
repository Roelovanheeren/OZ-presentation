15.1 Implementation Roadmap

Implementation Roadmap

Successful OZ investment implementation requires systematic approach encompassing investment strategy development, professional team assembly, regulatory compliance establishment, and ongoing portfolio management.

Professional Network Assembly

Engage specialized professionals including OZ-experienced tax counsel, qualified fund administrators, experienced sponsors, and compliance specialists. The complexity of OZ investing necessitates sophisticated professional support infrastructure.

Action Items

1. Conduct comprehensive portfolio analysis and gain harvesting strategy
2. Evaluate OZ investment opportunities and sponsor capabilities
3. Engage specialized tax and legal counsel for structure optimization
4. Develop investment policy and allocation framework
5. Execute investment strategy with appropriate compliance monitoring

Ongoing Education

The OZ regulatory environment continues to evolve with new guidance, market developments, and best practices. Investors should maintain current knowledge through professional networks, industry publications, and educational programs.

Market Intelligence

Success requires ongoing market intelligence covering regulatory developments, market trends, sponsor performance, and emerging opportunities. Professional networks and industry relationships provide competitive advantages in opportunity identification and execution.
