2.3 Market Evolution

Regulatory Framework

The Treasury Department and IRS have issued comprehensive regulations providing detailed guidance on qualification requirements, compliance obligations, and reporting standards.

Timeline Considerations

Investors have 180 days from the date of a qualifying capital gain to invest in a QOF to obtain tax benefits. The 180-day period begins when the gain would be recognized for federal tax purposes, providing flexibility for tax planning.

Program Duration

While Opportunity Zones maintain their designation through December 31, 2028, investors can continue to benefit from the tax advantages on existing investments held beyond this date. The program's design incentivizes long-term investment horizons aligned with community development goals.

Market Evolution

The OZ market has matured significantly since 2018, with institutional sponsors, fund managers, and investors developing sophisticated strategies and operational frameworks. This evolution has created more accessible investment opportunities for qualified investors.
