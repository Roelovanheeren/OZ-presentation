1. Executive Summary (cont.)

Market Size & Opportunity

The Opportunity Zone program encompasses 8,764 designated census tracts across all 50 states, territories, and the District of Columbia. These zones house approximately 35 million residents and have attracted over $75 billion in announced investments since program inception.

$6T+ Unrealized Gains Available

Investment Thesis

The program's unique tax structure creates powerful incentives for long-term capital deployment in underserved communities. The elimination of capital gains tax on appreciation for 10+ year holds represents an unprecedented wealth creation opportunity for patient capital strategies.

Phoenix Market Focus

Phoenix emerges as a tier-one OZ market with 149 designated zones, rapid population growth, economic diversification, and pro-business regulatory environment. The metropolitan area's demographic trends and development pipeline create exceptional investment opportunities across multiple asset classes.
