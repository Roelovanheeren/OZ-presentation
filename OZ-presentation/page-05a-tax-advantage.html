3. The Tax Advantage Framework

The Opportunity Zone tax framework provides three distinct benefits that can significantly enhance after-tax returns for qualified investments. Understanding these benefits is crucial for optimizing investment structures and timing.

Tax Deferral

Immediate Benefit: Capital gains invested in Opportunity Zones are deferred until December 31, 2026, or until the investment is sold, whichever comes first.

Cash Flow Impact: This deferral provides immediate liquidity benefits and allows for compound growth on the deferred tax liability.

Tax Reduction

5-Year Hold: 10% reduction in deferred gains

7-Year Hold: 15% reduction in deferred gains

Maximum Benefit: Achieved by holding until December 31, 2026
