4.1 Tax Benefits Overview

Three-Tier Tax Benefit Structure

The OZ tax framework provides unprecedented tax advantages through a carefully structured three-tier system designed to incentivize long-term capital deployment in underserved communities.

0% Tax Rate on 10+ Year Appreciation

Deferral Benefits

Capital gains invested in QOFs receive immediate deferral until December 31, 2026, or earlier disposition. This deferral creates powerful cash flow benefits and compound growth opportunities on the deferred tax liability, effectively providing an interest-free loan from the government.

Reduction Benefits

Long-term holds receive step-up basis adjustments: 10% after 5 years, additional 5% after 7 years (total 15%). These benefits maximize value for investments made before December 31, 2021, creating urgency for qualified gain deployment.

Elimination Benefits

The most powerful benefit eliminates all capital gains tax on appreciation for investments held 10+ years. This permanent exclusion applies to unlimited appreciation, creating extraordinary wealth-building opportunities for patient capital strategies.
