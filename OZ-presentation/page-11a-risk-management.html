10.2 Risk Mitigation Strategies

Operational Excellence

Development and operating risks require experienced management teams, comprehensive insurance coverage, and active asset management.

Best Practices

• Professional sponsor selection
• Comprehensive insurance programs
• Active compliance monitoring
• Regular performance reviews

Portfolio Diversification

Geographic and asset class diversification across multiple OZ investments reduces concentration risk while maintaining tax benefits.

Professional Support

Specialized legal, tax, and operational expertise provides ongoing risk monitoring and mitigation capabilities throughout the investment lifecycle.
