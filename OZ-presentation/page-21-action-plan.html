15. Next Steps & Resources (cont.)

Investment Strategy Development

Develop comprehensive investment strategy addressing risk tolerance, return objectives, liquidity needs, and tax planning goals. The strategy should integrate OZ investments within overall portfolio context while maximizing tax efficiency.

Portfolio Integration

OZ investments should complement existing portfolio allocations while providing diversification benefits and tax alpha generation. Consider correlation with existing holdings, liquidity requirements, and overall risk management objectives.

Performance Monitoring

Establish comprehensive monitoring systems tracking investment performance, regulatory compliance, and tax benefit optimization. Regular portfolio reviews ensure continued alignment with investment objectives and regulatory requirements.

Wealth Planning Integration

Coordinate OZ investments with broader wealth planning strategies including estate planning, charitable giving, and intergenerational wealth transfer. The tax benefits create unique planning opportunities for sophisticated investors.
