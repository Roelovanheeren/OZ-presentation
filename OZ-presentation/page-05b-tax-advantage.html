Tax-Free Appreciation

The most significant benefit is the complete elimination of capital gains tax on appreciation for investments held for 10 or more years. This benefit applies to the appreciation above the original deferred gain amount.

To maximize benefits, investors should consider the December 31, 2026 deadline for tax reduction benefits. Investments made before 2022 can achieve the full 15% reduction, while later investments may only achieve partial benefits.
