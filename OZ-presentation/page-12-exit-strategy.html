11. Due Diligence Process

Enhanced Due Diligence Framework

OZ investments require enhanced due diligence processes addressing traditional investment criteria plus regulatory compliance, tax optimization, and operational complexity. The framework must evaluate both investment merit and regulatory qualification.

Regulatory Due Diligence

Comprehensive analysis of QOF structure, asset qualification, compliance procedures, and ongoing monitoring capabilities. Legal opinions on regulatory positions and tax treatment are essential components of the due diligence process.

Due Diligence Checklist

• Market Analysis and Demographics
• Sponsor Track Record and Capabilities
• Financial Projections and Assumptions
• Regulatory Compliance and Legal Structure
• Risk Assessment and Mitigation Strategies

Operational Due Diligence

Assessment of sponsor capabilities, development experience, asset management expertise, and operational infrastructure. The complexity of OZ compliance requires specialized operational capabilities and professional service provider networks.

Financial Due Diligence

Analysis of projected returns, cash flow models, exit assumptions, and sensitivity analysis incorporating tax benefits. Financial projections must reflect both pre-tax performance and after-tax benefits to properly evaluate investment merit.
