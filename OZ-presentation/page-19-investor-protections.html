14.2 Investor Protections

Structural Safeguards

Institutional QOFs typically include investor-friendly governance structures, reporting requirements, and alignment mechanisms.

Key Protections

• Independent oversight boards
• Regular performance reporting
• Sponsor co-investment requirements

Regulatory Oversight

Professional compliance monitoring and regulatory oversight provide additional layers of investor protection and risk mitigation.

Legal Recourse

Comprehensive legal documentation establishes clear rights, responsibilities, and dispute resolution mechanisms for all parties.
