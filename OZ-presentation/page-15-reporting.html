12.3 Reporting Standards

Annual Filings

QOFs must file Form 8996 annually to report compliance with asset tests and other regulatory requirements.

Required Forms

• Form 8996: QOF Annual Filing
• Form 8997: Investor Elections

Investor Reporting

Investors file Form 8997 to report OZ investments and calculate deferred gains and basis adjustments.

Record Keeping

Comprehensive documentation including asset allocation, improvement activities, and business operations compliance.
