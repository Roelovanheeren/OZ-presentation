12.1 Legal Framework

Federal Framework

The OZ program is established under federal law, providing consistent tax benefits across all states and territories.

Key Regulations

• IRC Section 1400Z-1 & 1400Z-2
• Treasury Regulations
• IRS Forms 8996 & 8997

State Conformity

Most states conform to federal OZ provisions, though California, Massachusetts, and North Carolina have exceptions requiring careful planning.

Professional Counsel

Legal counsel is essential for structuring investments, ensuring compliance, and maximizing tax benefits.
