13.1 Performance Metrics

Return Measurement

OZ investments require specialized performance measurement incorporating tax benefits alongside traditional return metrics.

IRR+ After-Tax Enhancement

Key Performance Indicators

• Pre-tax IRR and cash-on-cash returns
• After-tax IRR including all tax benefits
• Compliance status and regulatory health
• Asset appreciation and valuation growth

Reporting Standards

Institutional QOFs typically provide quarterly reporting including financial performance, compliance status, and market updates.
